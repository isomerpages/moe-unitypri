<h3><strong>Badminton</strong></h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Programmes/2023/CCA/Badminton.jpg">
</div>
<p>The Badminton CCA provides opportunities for students to learn and apply
the fundamental sports skills of net games such as serving, receiving and
returning the shuttle cock to the opponent. &nbsp;Students will be taught
concepts such as depth and width play and how to attack and defend a space.
&nbsp;Basic rules of the game will progressively be taught and students
will learn to apply them in the game. &nbsp;Training will include basic
footwork, stamina building, agility and coordination exercises under the
close supervision of the teachers. &nbsp;Through the singles and doubles
games, students will also learn values such as sportsmanship, fairplay,
discipline and teamwork.</p>

will include basic ball skills, stamina building, agility and coordination

students will also learn values such as sportsmanship, fair play, teamwork
and discipline.</p>

<h3><strong>Floorball</strong></h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Programmes/2023/CCA/Floorball.jpg">
</div>
<p>The Floorball CCA provides opportunities for students to learn and apply
the fundamental sports skills such as passing, dribbling and shooting using
a long handed implement - the floorball stick. &nbsp;It is somewhat similar
to traditional hockey game but played in a smaller playing area and in
a rink.&nbsp; Students will be taught concepts such as moving into space,
attacking and defending. &nbsp;They will also learn the rules and progressively
apply them in the game. &nbsp;Training will include basic stick handling
skills, stamina building, agility and coordination exercises under the
close supervision of the teachers and coach. &nbsp;The students will also
learn values such as sportsmanship, fair play, teamwork and discipline.</p>

<h3><strong>Netball</strong></h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Programmes/2023/CCA/Netball.jpg">
</div>
<p>The Netball CCA provides opportunities for our girls to participate in
a ball game. &nbsp;Students will apply fundamental sports skills such as
throwing and catching, pivoting and shooting into the net.&nbsp; Students
will be taught concepts such as moving into space, attacking and defending.
They will also learn the complex rules in Netball and progressively apply
them in the game. &nbsp;Training will include basic ball skills, footwork,
stamina building, agility and coordination exercises under the close supervision
of the teachers and coach. &nbsp;Through the games, students will learn
values such as sportsmanship, fair play, teamwork and discipline.&nbsp;</p>
