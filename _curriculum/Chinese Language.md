### **<u>Vision 愿景</u>**


Creative thinkers with a passion for Chinese Language and Culture  
富创意的思考者，热衷母语和文化

### **<u>Mission 使命</u>**

Nurture students to be passionate about Chinese Language and Culture; Respect and appreciate cultural differences among different races  
培育热爱母语、尊重文化差异的学生
